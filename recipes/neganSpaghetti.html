Negan's Spaghetti

Description

Here's some context for all the uncultured people out there who have never seen The Walking Dead. (just kidding, it's okay you're still cultured)

Long story short, everyone's favourite character, Negan, arrives at Alexandria in Season 7, Episode 8. He does the great deed of feeding Carl Grimes (Rick's son) spaghetti!

And boy did it look good! So, I know what you're wondering: how do you make it?

Well here you go:

Ingredients

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper/chilli flakes
  • salt
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Start preparing the sauce by throwing some oil into the pan, and fying the garlic
  2. Then chuck in the tomato paste and chilli flakes
  3. Once that is fragrant and dark, add in your canned tomatoes and season with salt
  4. Leave the sauce to simmer while you cook the spaghetti
  5. Toss the sauce and the spaghetti together

And voila you have Negan Smith's famous Spaghetti all'Arrabbiata
